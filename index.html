About me

Sur moi

I am a Canadian second year computer science student at the University of Victoria with 11 years of programming experience and a proficiency in Python, although I pride myself on my ability to learn new technology extremely fast. I speak English natively and French fluently, have planned and taught non-profit programming courses to elementary students, developed educational tools used by several different schools, and have professional work experience as a software developer.
